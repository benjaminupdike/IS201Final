Strong Roots, Living to Serve About Me

I was born in May of 2000 to two wonderful parents who were strong in the gospel of Jesus Christ. They taught me to work, to love God and to enjoy life! I spent the majority of my childhood in San Antonio, Texas where I participated in marching band, scouting, and academic decathlon. When I graduated, I served a mission to Tacoma, Washington where I learned how to love, lead, and teach the gospel.

Now that I have returned, I am attending BYU where I am studying Global Supply Chain Management and am a drum major in the cougar marching band. I will soon begin the next chapter of my life by marrying the love of my life at the end of the semester!

I really have been blessed thus far because of my relationship with Jesus Christ. Watch the video below for a touching message about Him!
